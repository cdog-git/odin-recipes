Pork Sandwich

Description

Feast your eyes on the greatest pork sandwich recipe known in the history of the world! With fresh buns, marinated pulled pork, and the addition of other sides of your choice, you're sure to love this recipe! Try out this easy recipe today with your family and friends and make sure to check out our other recipes, thank you!

Ingredients

  • Bread buns
  • Pulled pork
  • Sauce (marinate)
  • Spices, salts, or oils of your choice (for the pork)
  • Sides of your choice!

Steps

  1. Cook your pork slow and steady for 20 minutes (make sure to use a generous amount of your marinate)
  2. Prep your buns (up to your personal preference, but we recommend toasting with a bit of butter!)
  3. After your pork is finished, pull it! (make sure the consistency of the pork is stringy and tender)
  4. After your buns are prepped, assemble the sandwich and add sauces and seasonings to your liking!
  5. Plate your sandwich with your desired sides and enjoy!
