Bacon Sandwich

Description

And now for one of the best sandwiches ever, period, the bacon sandwich! Notice the crispy strips of smoked bacon, the comforting scent of the bread, as well as the taste of the ingredients of your choice, there's nothing stopping you from crafting to your heart's desire with this recipe! Try it out today with your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • Bread (any type you'd like!)
  • Bacon strips (as many and whatever kind you'd like!)
  • Other ingredients of your choice! (lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sauces, etc)

Steps

  1. Cook your bacon strips (we recommend 4-5 if you're feeling yourself a crispy experience :->)
  2. Prep your bread slices (you can do so however you'd like; we like to toast our slices just slightly to add a nice crisp!)
  3. Assemble your sandwich, adding in your other tasty ingredients!
  4. Plate and serve up your fantastic bacon sandwich!

And there you have it! Odin Recipes' spectacular bacon sandwich! We hope you enjoyed this recipe and will visit us again some time soon. Until then, happy eating and cooking!
